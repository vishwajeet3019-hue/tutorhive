Board preparation timeline for Class 12 maths

July–November: Chapter coverage one by one, with practice after each chapter. December–January: Revision of all chapters and begin previous years' papers. February onwards: Full papers under timed conditions with tutor review. This structure produces consistent board performance — not cramming results.

FAQs

Which chapters carry the most marks in Class 12 maths boards?

Calculus carries approximately 44 marks out of 80. Vectors and 3D Geometry carry 17 marks. These two areas together account for over 75% of the board paper.

When should a Class 12 student start maths tuition?

At the start of Class 12 — July or August. Students who start in November or December have insufficient time to build concept understanding in Calculus.

Can a student who scored low in Class 11 still do well in Class 12 boards?

Yes — but Class 11 gaps must be identified and closed first. The first 4–6 weeks of Class 12 tuition often focus on bridging those gaps before new chapters begin.
