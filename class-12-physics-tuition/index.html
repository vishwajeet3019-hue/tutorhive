What Class 12 physics tuition covers

CBSE Class 12 physics has 14 chapters. Tuition is structured around board paper weightage so the highest-mark chapters get proportional depth.

Electricity & Magnetism — 33 marks

The largest section of Class 12 physics boards

  • Electric Charges, Fields, Potential — Gauss's law and applications
  • Current Electricity — Ohm's law, Kirchhoff's laws, Wheatstone bridge
  • Moving Charges and Magnetism — force on a current, magnetic field
  • Electromagnetic Induction and Alternating Current
Optics & Modern Physics — 30 marks

High-scoring sections with learnable patterns

  • Ray Optics — mirrors, lenses, refraction, optical instruments
  • Wave Optics — interference, diffraction, polarisation
  • Dual Nature of Radiation — photoelectric effect
  • Atoms, Nuclei, and Semiconductors — Modern Physics

FAQs

Which chapters carry the most marks in Class 12 physics boards?

Electricity and Magnetism (33 marks) and Optics (18 marks) together account for over 60% of the paper. These must be thoroughly prepared.

What are the most common mistakes in Class 12 physics board exams?

Not drawing diagrams before solving, wrong sign conventions in optics, incomplete derivations, and leaving Modern Physics unattempted. Modern Physics carries 12 marks and is very learnable.

Can a student who found Class 11 physics hard still do well in Class 12?

Yes. Class 12 physics — Electrostatics, Optics, Modern Physics — does not build on Class 11 mechanics directly. Students can start fresh with the right approach.
