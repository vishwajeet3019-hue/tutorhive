Custom website address

Use your own domain

If you already own a domain, add it here. Parents can then visit your website using your own address, like www.yourinstitute.com.

1
Save your domain here

Example: www.yourinstitute.com. Keep using your TutorHive preview link until this is connected.

2
Open where you bought the domain

Go to GoDaddy, Hostinger, Namecheap, Google Domains, or your domain provider. Open the DNS or Manage DNS page.

3
Add one CNAME record

Name/Host: www
Points to/Value: tutorhive-vxdj.onrender.com

After saving the record, click Check Connection. It can take a few minutes, sometimes up to 24 hours.
