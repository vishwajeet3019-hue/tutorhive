How to Choose Online Tuition for Your Child

The best online tuition is not just about finding a teacher. It is about finding the right pace, format, curriculum alignment, and communication style for your child.

Start with the real problem

Before choosing tuition, identify what your child actually needs. Some students need concept clarity, some need regular revision, some need exam practice, and some mainly need confidence to ask doubts without fear.

Check whether the tutor adapts to your child

A good online tutor should not teach every student in the same way. Ask whether lessons can follow your child's school chapters, current level, and learning pace. This matters especially for students in Classes 1-10, where small gaps can become bigger over time.

Choose the right class format

A 1:1 class is useful when a child needs personal attention, slower explanation, or focused doubt clearing. A small group class can work well when the student is comfortable learning with peers and needs a steady weekly rhythm.

Look for parent visibility

Parents should know what happened in class, what improved, and what still needs practice. Clear updates help you understand whether tuition is actually supporting progress.

Always try a demo first

A demo class helps you check tutor fit, teaching style, comfort level, and whether your child responds well. TutorHive offers online tuition for Classes 1-10 across subjects like maths, science, and English.
