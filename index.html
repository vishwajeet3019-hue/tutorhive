Frequently asked by parents

Which classes does TutorHive support?

TutorHive supports school students from Classes 1 to 10 with online tuition built around each child's current level.

Can my child take only maths or science tuition?

Yes. Parents can choose subject-specific support such as maths tuition, science tuition, English tuition, or broader CBSE support.

How do I know if the tutor is right for my child?

Start with a Free Demo Class, discuss your child's needs, and review the tutor's teaching approach before continuing.

Do parents receive updates?

Yes. TutorHive is designed around parent trust, with clear communication, progress visibility, and responsive support.

Is the trial class really free?

Yes. The 30-minute trial helps you understand tutor fit, teaching style, and your child's learning needs before you decide on regular classes.

How are classes matched to my child's school board?

We ask for the class, subject, curriculum, current chapters, and test schedule so tutoring can follow school expectations instead of a generic worksheet plan.

Can classes help before exams or only for regular learning?

Both. Students can use TutorHive for steady weekly learning, focused revision before tests, or targeted doubt-clearing when a chapter feels stuck.

What happens after I submit the form?

We review your child's details and reply with suitable demo options, timing availability, and next steps for the subject or curriculum you selected.
