Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions parents usually ask before booking a demo class.

Which curricula does TutorHive support?

TutorHive currently focuses on CBSE and ICSE-aligned tuition for Classes 1-10, with chapter planning based on the student's school syllabus.

Who teaches my child?

Students learn from trained peer tutor interns who are screened for subject knowledge, communication, reliability, and comfort with younger learners.

How do parents track progress?

Parents receive visibility into attendance, homework status, and session feedback reports so they know what improved and what needs more practice.

Do you publish fixed pricing?

We share pricing after understanding your child's class, subject needs, preferred frequency, and learning goals. Parents can request a quote through the contact form.

Can we try a class first?

Yes. Book a Free Demo to meet the tutor, discuss your child's needs, and decide whether TutorHive is the right fit.

Is student data private?

TutorHive only collects learning and contact details needed to run classes and support parents. We do not sell parent or student data.
